\recipe{Osmanthus Sticky Rice Soup}
{Cody Cao}
{Fav dessert soup growing up in Syxhou}
{ % Ingredients Command
    \ingredients{

\item Adzuki Bean (red beans) , 200g
\item Fermented Glutinous Rice Liquid, 100g
\item Dried Osmanthus, 20g
\item Mini Glutinous Rice Balls,200g
\item Sugar, to taste

}
}
{ % Instructions Command
    \instructions{
\item Soak dried red beans in water overnight
\item The next day, place soaked beans, and a new batch of clear water in a large pot and bring to a boil over high heat. After boil, bring down to medium low heat for about 1 hr so that the beans are cooked till semi-mushy. 
\item When the red beans are almost done, start a different pot and cook ready-to-cook rice balls. drain.
\item Add fermented rice liquid and cooked rice balls for 1-2 minutes so the flavors blend. 
\item Serve while hot with dried osmanthus garnished on top and sugar to taste. 

    
    }
}
